Environmental Fun Facts For The Kids

Smencils -Gourmet Scented Pencils are made from
100% recycled newspapers

Being responsible for the environment is an important part of doing business in today's world. That is why the materials and processes that go into making Smencils are healthy for the environment, so you can fundraise in confidence.

 

The Original Smencil is an environmental friendly, GO GREEN Fundraising product!

The manufacture of Gourmet Smencils uses recycled newspapers, environmentally friendly fragrances, and biodegradable erasers. Even the tube each Smencils comes in and the plastic bucket are made of biodegradable plastic made from corn.

Here are some facts about the manufacturing of Smencil Products:

  • Sheets of 100% recycled newspaper are rolled tightly around a #2 graphite-writing core until pencils of typical thickness are formed.
  • Then they are hardened, allowing them to be sharpened just like a wood pencil.
  • Next the Gourmet Smencil is soaked with an environmentally friendly liquid gourmet scent.
  • Once dry, the biodegradable erasers are attached and flavor sticker is wrapped around the Smencil to identify what Scent was infused into the Smencil.

(Each Smencil comes packaged in a re-sealable, biodegradable plastic tube.)

Environmental facts about Go-Green Smencils Fundraising:
An average sized cedar tree will make about 172,000 wood pencils.
 		   The average tree can filter about 60 pounds of air pollution per year.
172,000 Smencils can be made from 430 newspapers.   If everyone in the US recycled just 10% of their newspapers, we would save about 25 million trees each year.
Recycling a single run of the Sunday New York Times saves about 75,000 trees.
