Frequently Asked Questions about cookie dough delivery:
 
How long will it take to receive my cookie dough once I place my order?
Turnaround time is 3-4 weeks after we receive your order.

How does the cookie dough come?
Cookie dough will be packed with dry ice, shrink wrapped on a pallet.  (This does not apply if your location is in the Houston, Dallas/Ft. Worth, Austin or San Antonio metropolitan areas.)

How long will my cookie dough stay cool after I receive my order?
Cookie dough will stay cool 6 to 8 hours after your delivery.

How will I be notified about my delivery?
Your Fund-raising Consultant will notify you in advance of the actual ship date and projected arrival date.  You will have a tracking number and the phone number of the trucking company to track your order.

Do I need cold storage to receive my cookie dough?
No, cookie dough will arrive packed in dry ice. It can be left out without refrigeration for 10 to 12 hours if kept in the same boxes or, if it's not in dry ice, it can be left out at room temperature for 6 to 8 hours.

How many volunteers will I need?
We recommend 1 helper per 200 tubs.  You will need at least 1 helper for deliveries of less than 200 tubs.
