What is the Value Card?

Simply put, the Value Card is the size, shape and thickness of a standard credit card.  On the back are (12) to (15) popular local merchants who have agreed to participate by sponsoring a discount valid for one full year of unlimited use.  The front of the card displays your group's name, logo and colors. These custom cards are a great way to strengthen your group's identity and fundraising project.

The Value Card benefits:

  • Group profits up to 55%
  • Popular name brand merchants make this card easy to sell.
  • No Upfront Money required to get started.
  • No more long hours spent delivering product or collecting money.
  • Your supporters save money all year long.
